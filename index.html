The Problem We're Solving

Widespread Impact

In India, approximately 63 million people (6% of the population) suffer from significant auditory loss

Social Barriers

This community faces barriers in education, employment, and daily interactions due to communication challenges

Limited Solutions

Existing solutions are often expensive, not real-time, or require specialized hardware, limiting widespread adoption

Urgent Need

There's a pressing need for an accessible, affordable, and efficient communication tool to bridge this gap and promote inclusivity

Key Features

Translation

Real-time sign language to speech/text conversion using advanced AI and machine learning

Language Support

Support for Indian Sign Language (ISL) and major regional spoken languages

Offline Accessibility

Functionality in areas with limited connectivity, optimized for low-bandwidth areas

Impact and Market Potential

Innovation

First comprehensive, real-time sign language translation platform tailored for Indian users with novel use of AI for real-world scenarios

Market Size

Targeting 63 million hearing-impaired individuals in India with an estimated market size of $500 million by 2047

Expansion Potential

Opportunity for expansion to other South Asian countries with similar communication needs

Business Model

Freemium model with enterprise licenses for businesses and educational institutions
